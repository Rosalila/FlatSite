The Mana World

The Mana World is the original project. It has three cities full of adventures: Hurnscald a magical forest, Tulimshar a wild desert and Nivalis a cold mountain. The game has a deep gameplay letting you play until level +90 with multiple quests. The game is free (as in freedom) and everyone can play and contribute to the game development.

  • Official site: TheManaWorld.org
  • Facebook: Facebook.com/TheManaWorld
  • Steam: The Mana World's greenlight

The Dogecoin District

The Dogecoin District is located in the moon. You can use the Rocket NPCs to travel between The Mana World, The Dogecoin District and Tales of the Balck Forest. Here you can find NPCs with dogecoin integration such as a Doge ATM, a Fuacet or a Dogecoin Arena.

Tales of the Black Forest

This is brand new adventure The Shibe World team is developing. We expect to have an exciting story whit pvp gamplay and Dogecoin integration.

Doge ATM

Every character has a Dogecoin wallet and address in-game. You use the ATM to deposit, withdraw and send doges to other players.

Doge arena

Bet 5 doges to enter the PVP arena. The last man standing receives the pot. The server gets 1Ð per participant.
